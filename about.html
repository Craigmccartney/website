Who We Are

We are Code Nation, the UK's fastest growing Software Development and Cyber apprenticeship provider and award-winning coding school offering education with a difference.

We believe that great tech talent can come from anywhere and our courses are open to everyone! We have a proven track record of taking candidates with zero coding skills to employable talent significantly faster than traditional learning environments.

Working with our partner businesses, we offer short courses and immersive learning bootcamps that teach a range of core digital skills including Software Development and Cyber Security. We offer remote learning available and work closely with the government to provide free funding for the majority of our courses
